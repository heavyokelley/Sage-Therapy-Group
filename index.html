Thinking about therapy?

We get that even considering professional counseling is a big, sometimes scary step. Our goal is to make taking that step as easy and accepting as possible while introducing the patient to the benefits of professional therapy.

Understand Yourself

By spending time with a professional counselor, the patient can gain insight into their own lives, helping them explain confusing thoughts, behaviors or feeling.

Stop Self-Defeating

Patients learn to identify and manage thoughts and actions that hold back their growth, giving them the ability to truly change and develop.

Be more Confident

People who participate in professional therapy find that they feel better prepared (and better supported!) to handle daily life and achieve personal goals.

Manage Stress Better

Through talk therapy, patients find what triggers their stress and learn how to better handle daily or life-changing challenges.

Sage is professional.

As a professional counseling and psychotherapy service, we use a non-judgemental approach to help patients resolve emotional concerns and work towards a more accepting view of themselves. We meet patients half-way, using tailored techniques to encourage them on their journey of self-exploration and change. We can help most people with anything they may need support with, but we specialize in stress and anger management, child and adolescent behavior, interpersonal relationship skills, parenting skill and father abandonment issues.

Sekou Fraser is a licensed professional counselor with over fifteen years of counseling, educational and mental health experience. He is adept at helping individuals overcome personal challenges and is comfortable working with patients from all walks of life.
